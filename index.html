Who We Are

BORN ON
THE ROAD

Throttle Trekkers is a passionate motorcycle community united by a shared love for the open road, wild terrains, and the brotherhood that forms when engines roar together. We ride not just to reach a destination — we ride to feel alive.

From weekend canyon runs to multi-day Himalayan expeditions, every ride tells a story. Every rider carries a piece of that story with them. Our crest says it all — a trekker on foot, a rider on dirt, and mountains that dare you to conquer them.

🤝

Brotherhood

Every rider is family. We look out for each other on and off the road.

🪖

Safety First

🗺️

Exploration

New roads, new horizons. Every trail is an invitation.

How We Ride

RIDER
CODE

01

Gear Up Every Time

Helmet, gloves, jacket — no exceptions. Riding gear is a sign of respect for your life and your crew's peace of mind.

02

No Rider Left Behind

We ride as a pack and finish as a pack. Watch your mirrors, check on the rider behind you.

03

Respect the Road

Follow traffic rules. Respect pedestrians, animals, and other vehicles. Our reputation rides on the road with us.

04

Zero Tolerance for Alcohol

Not a drop before a ride. We ride clear-headed or we don't ride at all.

05

Community Over Ego

No showboating, no reckless overtakes. We ride to enjoy and connect — not to impress.
